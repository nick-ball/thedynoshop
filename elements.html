Why The Dyno Shop?

Here at our shop, you will experience professionalism like you have never seen before. The owner/operator is named Jake. He runs this shop and he has an extensive background in customer satisfaction first, and motorcycles second. Jake lives, eats and breaths all things motorsports. No matter what your knowledge level is, when you talk about the work you need done on your bike, you will find enjoyment from listening to Jake's detailed explanations of the work that he has done to all sorts of bikes, and the way he will do the work you need. Our shop has an open layout, so you can bring your vehicle by and get a firsthand look at the shop and all of the machinery we have that all serves one purpose - to make your motorcycle better.

We don't charge an arm and a leg

Instead of going to the dealership where you got the bike, come to our shop for service and repairs. You will see the cost difference immediately and you will be pleasantly surprised by how much less we charge. You provide the parts, so the only thing we charge for is the cost of labor. In the meantime, you can watch our shop's social media pages for picture updates on your machine while it is being worked on.

We are fast & friendly

If you bring in your machine to the shop for an oil change, tire change, and dyno flash and tune all at once, you can expect to be back in the saddle in a few short days. Jake does everything he can to work meticulously on the project in front of him from sunrise to sunset, until it is finished. Once the bike is ready for pickup, you can have any questions about the services preformed answered by in a friendly and understanding manner by Jake himself.
